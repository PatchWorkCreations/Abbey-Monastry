Meditation Garden of Truth & Reconciliation

Listen to Tour Audio:

Mepkin Abbey has a Meditation Garden of Truth and Reconciliation, which is a significant addition to their grounds. This garden is a project focusing on reconciliation and includes a sculpture entitled “Thy Father’s Hand,” featuring the crucified Christ in the Hand of God. The garden incorporates the Henry Laurens family graveyard, allowing the complete history of the enslaved at Mepkin to be part of this permanent installation. It’s designed as a space for reflection, featuring a path with informative elements for visitors.
