Memorial Garden

Listen to Tour Audio:

Mepkin Abbey is in the process of developing a new Memorial Garden near the entrance to the African-American cemetery. This garden is envisioned as an oasis of prayer and tranquility, inviting visitors to take a contemplative stroll and reflect. The plan includes the planting of trees and flowers, enhancing the overall serene atmosphere. Furthermore, there is an opportunity for individuals to acquire a memorial block engraved with the name of a loved one as a lasting form of remembrance. For more details on this project and how to purchase a memorial block, please refer to Mepkin Abbey's website. This new addition to Mepkin Abbey will offer a serene space for visitors to cherish the memory of their loved ones in a peaceful setting.
