Memorial Garden

Listen to Tour Audio:

Mepkin Abbey is developing a new Memorial Garden near the entrance to the African-American cemetery. This garden is intended to be an oasis of prayer and quiet, where visitors can walk around and reflect. The plan includes planting trees and flowers, enhancing the tranquil atmosphere. Additionally, there’s an opportunity for individuals to purchase a block engraved with the name of a loved one as a form of remembrance. More details about this project and how to purchase a memorial block will be available on Mepkin Abbey’s website. This addition to Mepkin Abbey will provide a serene space for visitors to remember and honor their loved ones in a peaceful setting.
