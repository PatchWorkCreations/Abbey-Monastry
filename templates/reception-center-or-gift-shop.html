Reception Center / Gift Shop

Listen to Tour Audio:

The Mepkin Abbey Reception Center/Gift Shop serves as a special place where visitors can immerse themselves in the monastic tradition. Within its walls, you'll discover an array of artistic works from local, national, and international monastics and artisans, showcasing a deep reverence for God's creation. Our selection encompasses books, religious art, and handcrafted items, all chosen for their spiritual and aesthetic value. It's a wonderful spot to find unique items that celebrate both monastic life and craftsmanship.
