Mepkin Abbey Botanical Garden

Listen to Tour Audio:

Mepkin Abbey is home to the Nancy Bryan Luce Gardens, a botanical garden that offers visitors a serene and contemplative environment. These gardens blend meticulously maintained landscapes with natural beauty, reflecting the monastery's commitment to harmonizing with nature. We extend an invitation to visitors to leisurely explore the gardens, basking in the tranquility and spiritual ambiance they provide. These gardens are an integral part of Mepkin Abbey's dedication to offering a space for reflection and prayer amidst the splendor of creation.
