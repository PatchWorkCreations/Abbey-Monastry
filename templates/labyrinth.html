Labyrinth

Listen to Tour Audio:

The Labyrinth Walk at Mepkin Abbey in Moncks Corner, South Carolina, offers a unique and profound spiritual experience. It serves as a space for contemplation and communion with nature, inviting visitors to embark on a personal journey of reflection and discovery. As eloquently described by a previous visitor, walking the labyrinth is a transformative experience, fostering a profound sense of mindfulness and a profound connection with the natural world. This setting provides a precious opportunity for individuals to engage in contemplative prayer and find inner peace amidst the enchanting beauty of the abbey's grounds.
