St. Clare Walkway

Listen to Tour Audio:

The St. Clare Walkway at Mepkin Abbey is an integral part of the tranquil and picturesque surroundings of the monastery. Nestled on the historic grounds of the Mepkin Plantation along the Cooper River, this walkway provides a peaceful haven for both contemplation and exploration. It is set amidst the natural beauty of the Abbey's surroundings, offering visitors an opportunity to bask in landscaped vistas, gardens, and scenic views. The St. Clare Walkway stands as an ideal setting for a reflective and serene journey.
