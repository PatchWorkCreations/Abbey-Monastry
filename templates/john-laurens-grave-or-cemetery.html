John Laurens Grave / Cemetery

Listen to Tour Audio:

John Laurens, an American soldier and statesman during the Revolutionary War, is laid to rest in the Mepkin Abbey Monastic Cemetery in Moncks Corner, Berkeley County, South Carolina. His grave is an integral part of the rich historical tapestry of Mepkin Abbey, underscoring the site's deep connection to American history.
