Mepkin Abbey Columbarium

The Columbarium at Mepkin Abbey offers a peaceful and sacred final resting place. It's situated within the monastery's grounds, aligning with the monastic community's values of tranquility and respect for the departed. This sacred space provides a serene environment for both the interred and their loved ones to connect with spirituality and nature.
